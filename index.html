Analysis of the impact of 3D folding of DNA on gene expression

This project was realised in the team of Dr. Olivier CUVIER (CBI, Toulouse, https://cbi-toulouse.fr/eng/equipe- cuvier), we explored the relation between 3D organisation and transcriptional regulation. To do so, we compared the genome-wide physical contact maps of 3D-disturbed-mutants in C.elegans.
